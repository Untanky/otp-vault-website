Data Privacy Statement for OTP Vault

Effective Date: August 7, 2025

  1. Overview

    OTP Vault (“the App”) provides one-time-password (OTP) generation entirely on your iOS device. We respect your privacy and have designed the App so that no secret or personal data ever leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself.

  2. Information Collected

    • OTP setup data you enter or scan by QR code:
      • Secret key (used to generate OTPs)
      • OTP parameters (period, code length, algorithm)
      • Label, issuer, account name (for your identification)
    • Camera access: used solely to scan QR codes for OTP setup. No photos or video are stored, transmitted or viewed by any person or server.

  3. How We Use Your Data

    All setup data is used only to generate one-time-passwords on your device. We do not transmit, sell, share or otherwise disclose any of your OTP secrets or identification fields to any third party.

  4. Data Storage & Security

    • Local-only storage: all information resides on your iPhone or iPad.
    • Keychain: secret keys and any sensitive parameters are stored in Apple’s secure Keychain, benefiting from hardware-backed encryption.
    • Backups: Keychain items may be included in encrypted iCloud or device backups if you have enabled those features. You control your backups via iOS Settings.

  5. Camera Usage

    • Permission: the App requests access to your camera only to scan QR codes for OTP setup.
    • Scope: scanning is performed in real time; no images or video frames are saved or transmitted. You may revoke camera permission at any time in Settings.

  6. Third-Party Services

    The App does not integrate analytics, crash-reporting, advertising or any other third-party SDK that collects user data.

  7. Your Choices & Rights

    • Data deletion: to remove all OTP entries, use the App’s “Delete All” function or uninstall the App from your device.
    • Camera revocation: disable camera access under iOS Settings > Privacy > Camera.
    • Backup control: manage iCloud or local backups under iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud or Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Backups.

  8. Children

    The App is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

  9. Changes to This Statement

    We may update this Privacy Statement when features change. The new version will be posted here with an updated “Effective Date.”
