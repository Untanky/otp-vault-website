Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about using OTP Vault with your accounts.

What is OTP Vault, exactly?

OTP Vault is a two‑factor authentication app that generates time‑based one‑time passwords (TOTP) for your online accounts. It’s private, fast, and designed for iOS.

Does it sync between my devices?

Yes. With iCloud, your encrypted vault can stay up to date across iPhone and iPad. Only your devices can decrypt your data.

Can I import from Google Authenticator or others?

Absolutely. Import by scanning QR codes or pasting otpauth:// URIs. Bulk import and CSV are supported where providers allow it.

What if I lose my phone?

Use your recovery methods: iCloud‑synced vault on a new device, recovery codes from each service, or an encrypted backup you keep safe.

Does OTP Vault support AutoFill?

Yes, OTP Vault integrates with iOS AutoFill to suggest your codes right from the keyboard, in apps and Safari.
