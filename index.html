Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about using OTP Vault with your accounts.

What is OTP Vault, exactly?

OTP Vault is a two‑factor authentication app that generates time‑based one‑time passwords (TOTP) for your online accounts. It’s private, fast, and designed for iOS.

One-time passwords help protect your account against phishing and password leaks by requiring a second password, makimg it harder to compromise accounts.

Why not use my password manager?

Many password managers now allow you to generate one-time passwords as well. While this is highly convenient it does not increase account security, as a compromise of your password manager means your account is no longer secure.

By using another one-time password app, you keep a separation between your authentication factors, increase security and resilience.

How does OTP Vault keep my accounts secure?

OTP Vault stores the secrets used to generate one-time passwords encrypted on your devices using the system's Keychain. The app does not communicate with any other systems. Backups can faciliated through encrypted iCloud backups.

How do I use OTP Vault?

When you setup MFA on one of your accounts, the service will provide you with a QR code. You can scan this code with the app and the one- time password will be added to OTP Vault.

Now, the one-time password associated with the account can be see anytime in the app and updates periodically. You can simply tap to copy it and paste it in the application again.
